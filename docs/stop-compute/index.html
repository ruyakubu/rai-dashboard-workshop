Shutdown

Be sure to shutdown the compute instance at the end of this workshop so you do not run out of credits.

  1. To shutdown the compute instance, click on the Compute icon on the left-hand side of the Azure ML studio.

  1. Under "Compute Instance", select the "cluster-cpu" instance. Then, click on the "Stop" button.
